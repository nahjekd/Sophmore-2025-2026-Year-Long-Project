About Us

Informing about our motivations, goals, and reasoning for creating this project

Our Mission

Our website is here to bring attention to any fossils that have made significant contributions to the history of the world in the past or just helped learn more about the types of dinosaurs that used to roam our country, specifically ones that were discovered here in Kentucky.

What We Do

We want anyone who may be interested in any of the fossils we have found or anyone who is interested in what we can find to join us in our discovery of the world in the past.
