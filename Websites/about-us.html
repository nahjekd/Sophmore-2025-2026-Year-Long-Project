Berenice

Berenice is a character in the webtoon "Nevermore", she isn't really shown much as of the current story. However she is in the group called the misfits, which is made up of 5 people that are usually causing problems in their class or school. Breaking rules they are supposed to because it causes the dean's problems and she doesn't like them.

Original story

In her original story by Edgar Allan Poe, Bernice is wedded with her cousin who tends to have a fixation on certain things while she has an illness where her body breaks down. One day he sees her teeth and has thoughts of pulling them out, later on she dies and all we read that he goes to her body and pulls out her teeth. The next day one of the servants said that her grave had been disturbed and that she was still alive. Next to him (the narrator) was a shovel with a box of 32 of her teeth.

Designs

School Uniform

She has dark skin, short curly hair, pearl earrings, with thick black eyebrows, her edges done, with a grey dress covered by a short black jacket.

Spectre (Strigoi)

In her spectre form called the Strigoi, Berenice becomes a vampire, she turns red, with big wings instead of arms, and multiple mouths across her body. She wears a black dress with marbles on the end, black fishnets, short curly hair with pearl necklaces on top, and black wings

Her Death

In her backstory, berenice is dressed up nicely, looking seemingly drunk, she darts in to the road and a car comes through crashing into her, killing her

Character Traits

Berenice is a very sociable person, she's not afraid to speak out or talk to anyone about anything. She also is very reliable when it comes down to it, everyone can always rely on her to do her part. Also very impulsive and mischievous, she acts without thinking and sometimes can lead her into trouble, she never thinks about the consequences and does anything she enjoys doing.

Character development

Unfortunately, so far in the story Berenice hasn't had much character development.
